The Awards

GTM AI Workshop · Three awards, chosen live. Not about winning — about celebrating what got built (and everyone leaves with something).

How this works. This isn't a leaderboard. After the demos the room votes (Mentimeter) and the mentors add their call — three awards, one build each. The point of the day is building and the network you leave with, so keep it light and loud. Every team also gets a goody bag regardless.

Made Claude Do What?!

The most surprising, creative or downright unexpected use of Claude — the "I didn't know you could do that" moment of the day.

Mentors' call — the build that made the room lean in.

Beautiful Disaster

The boldest swing — reached for the ambitious thing instead of the safe one. Celebrated whether or not it fully landed. Very on-brand for a hackathon.

Who tried the hardest thing in the room? Nerve over polish.

The Storyteller

Best demo — the team that made the room feel the problem and the fix in three minutes. A great system still has to be sold.

Room vote — the demo you'd replay for someone who wasn't there.

Everyone leaves with something

A goody bag for every participant — tote, notebook, and a little build kit (a nod to what the day's about). The winning teams pick a prize: the podcast, the build kit, or the game. No one goes home empty-handed.

Three awards for up to ~5 teams — deliberately fewer awards than teams, because it's a celebration, not a ranking. Trophies/prizes are silly and physical: people photograph them, and those photos are the post-event LinkedIn recap. (Superseded the earlier 5-award draft per the Jul 14 catch-up.)
