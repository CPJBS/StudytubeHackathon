The Awards

GTM AI Workshop · Every team should be able to win something — so nobody leaves empty-handed and everyone leaves photographed.

Facilitator note: announce these at the showcase, most-fun-last. Aim to give every team at least one. Trophies should be silly and physical — people photograph trophies, and those photos are your post-event LinkedIn recap.

The Closer

Best actual fix — the build we'd genuinely ship on Monday. The grand prize.

Made Claude Do WHAT?!

Most creative or unexpected use of Claude — the "I didn't know you could do that" moment.

Beautiful Disaster

Best thing that broke gloriously. Celebrates ambition and risk — the opposite of playing it safe.

The Reply Guy Brief 01

Outreach we'd actually answer — the sequence that beats the 0.4% baseline for real.

Beige Buster Brief 02

Most ruthless kill of corporate mush — the system that best held the voice across authors and languages.

The Whisperer Brief 03

Found the signal everyone else missed — saw through the noise to the insight that mattered.

Crowd Favourite

Audience-voted at the showcase. The room decides — pure popularity, no judges.

Fastest Bell

First team to ring the "it works" bell. Rewards momentum and getting something running early.

The Storyteller

Best 3-minute demo — the team that made us feel the problem and the fix, not just see it.

People's MVP Individual

The one person who unblocked everyone — helped other teams, kept the energy up. Not about their own build.

All awards fictional-company-agnostic and reusable for future workshops. Swap brief-specific names (Reply Guy / Beige Buster / Whisperer) if the briefs change.
