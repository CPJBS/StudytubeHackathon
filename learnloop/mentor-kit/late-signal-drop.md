## For mentors — what a good triage does

**ACC-010 (hot-mover):** BaFin audit + new CHRO with explicit modernisation mandate = forced evaluation window. Move this account from B-tier to A-list immediately; lead with regulatory confidence and audit-trail proof.

**ACC-023 (noise):** €15M capex is operational growth, not L&D trigger. Signal looks like progress but doesn't create training urgency. Don't move this account; note the capex timeline and watch for a _separate_ training signal once machinery deployment begins.

**ACC-043 (ambiguous):** Board transition typically freezes new tool investments 6–9 months. Pain point is real (food-safety + export training pressure), but timing is wrong now. Flag as "watch and wait"; circle back Q4 2026 after governance settles.
