# Current Working Assumptions — Pre-Task-Force Note

Circulated by Mila Verhoeven (RevOps) ahead of Thursday's task force, so everyone walks in already knowing where the rest of the room stands.

**Bram Hoekstra, CRO:** "Trial quality has dropped this year. Marketing's leads aren't what they used to be, and sales is stuck closing weaker prospects than a year ago."

**Nora Bakker, VP Marketing:** "Acquisition is healthy — we're bringing in the volume we always have. The leak is in week one. Once people land in the product, we lose them before they ever see what Loop can do. That's an activation problem, not ours."

**Iris Chen, Head of Product:** "The churn we're seeing is a sales-expectations problem. Deals get sold on capabilities Loop doesn't fully have yet, and customers churn when the product doesn't match the pitch."

**Daan Vermeulen, CEO — from last week's board slide:** "LinkedIn reach is at an all-time high. It's our best channel, and we should be doubling down on it going into next quarter."

**Finance:** "Trial-to-paid conversion has been stuck around 6% all year. The growth model is broken, and we need to be honest with the board about that."

Mila's line, attached to the note: "At most one of these can be true. The data pack is attached."
