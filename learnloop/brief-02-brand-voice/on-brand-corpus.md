## 17. Product update: Learn now flags expiring certifications before your auditor does
*Content type: Product-update note*

Compliance certifications expire quietly, usually right before the audit that checks them. Starting today, Learn flags any certification 60 days before it lapses, not the week after.

We built this after watching a customer's audit trail show three "compliant" employees whose certifications had actually expired two months earlier. The dashboard said green. The paperwork said otherwise. Nobody had noticed until the auditor did.

Now the flag shows up on the manager's dashboard and in a weekly digest, with the name, the certification, and the days remaining. No more finding out during the audit.

It's on for every Learn account by default. Turn it off in Settings > Compliance if your workflow already covers this some other way.
